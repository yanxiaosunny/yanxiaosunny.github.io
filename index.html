Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Professor James Cheng. Before coming to CUHK, I got my M.Phil. degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications under the supervision of Professor Ping Zhang, and my bachelor degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.

Research Interest

My interests lie at the intersection of database and machine learning, including similarity search, probabilistic algorithms, machine learning algorithms, and systems for large-scale machine learning
